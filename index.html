Visit Accra

Enjoy culture and heritage in this vibrant city in Ghana.

Top three activities to do in Accra

View artefacts about Ghana's Independence.

The Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park offers an in-depth history of the Sub-Saharan struggle for independence.

Attend a concert or musical performance.

The Ghana National Theatre is a multi-functional venue, which hosts concerts, dance, drama, exhibitions, and more.

Experience natural beauty and relaxation.

The Labadi Beach is a place for leisure, recreational activities, wildlife, and nature exploration.
